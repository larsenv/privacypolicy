Content Rights

Content and media rights for Project Countdown.

This page explains how the public site and app describe app assets, user-selected photos, curated history content, and support materials.

App and website assets

Project Countdown app icons, product text, and website design assets are provided for the Project Countdown public-information site and app support materials.

User-selected photos

If users select photos for event backgrounds, they are responsible for having the right to use those photos. The app stores selected photos locally for that user’s countdown experience.

History packs

Curated history-pack entries are intended as factual date-oriented content. Any concern about a history entry can be reported through support.

Support attachments

If a user sends screenshots or recordings to support, those materials are used to understand and respond to the support request.
