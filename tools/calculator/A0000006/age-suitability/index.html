Age Suitability

Age suitability information for Great Calculator.

Great Calculator is a calculator and utility app designed for general audiences. This page provides product-specific context for the optional Age Suitability URL in App Store Connect.

No account required No user-generated content No chat or social feed No unrestricted web browsing

Why this page exists

This page is designed to serve as the dedicated public URL for the optional Age Suitability field in App Store Connect.

Summary

General-audience utility app

What the app is

Great Calculator is a general-purpose calculator and utility app for iPhone and iPad covering math, graphing, geometry, time, conversion, finance, counting, and number-analysis workflows.

What it does not include

The app does not include user-generated content, messaging, chat, social networking, gambling, violent content, sexual content, or unrestricted in-app web browsing.

Accounts and advertising

No account is required for normal use, and the current release does not present in-app advertising or third-party analytics SDKs.

Network use

Most workflows are local. Network access is only used when the user explicitly requests exchange-rate data for currency conversion.

Age suitability details

How the app is intended to be used

General audiences

The app is suitable for people who need a calculator and conversion utility for school, work, planning, travel, and everyday math.

No open-content systems

Users cannot post public content, upload social posts, or browse arbitrary web pages within the app.

No chat or community layer

The app does not offer chat, comments, private messaging, profile systems, or community feeds.

No mature themes

The app is not built around mature themes, violence, sexual content, or chance-based monetized activities.

Note: Region-specific age ratings are displayed by Apple on the App Store. This page supports the optional Age Suitability URL field with product-specific public information.
