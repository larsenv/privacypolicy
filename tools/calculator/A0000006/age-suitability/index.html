Age suitability

Age suitability for the current release.

Great Calculator is a utility calculator app. The current release is designed for ordinary math, graphing, conversion, time, and finance workflows and does not include social networking, user-generated public content, gambling, unrestricted web browsing, or medical-treatment features.

Current expected rating posture

The repository’s age-rating worksheet expects a 4+ result for the current release unless the product scope changes, but the final rating must always come from the App Store Connect questionnaire completed for the submitted build.

  • No account creation or messaging system is required.
  • No advertising SDKs or ad-targeting flows are included.
  • No gambling, contest, or wagering features are included.
  • No health-treatment or medical-advice feature is exposed in the app target.
  • The optional online path is limited to user-requested exchange-rate lookup in the Money calculator.

Re-answer the App Store Connect age-rating questionnaire if future versions add ads, unrestricted web access, user-generated content, chat, medical content, gambling-related content, or other higher-sensitivity features.
