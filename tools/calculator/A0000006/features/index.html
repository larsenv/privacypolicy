Features

Build timelines around the dates that matter.

Project Milepost is a focused event and milestone tracker for iPhone and iPad.

Personal events

Create events, goals, anniversaries, memories, birthdays, and deadlines with optional notes and optional local background photos.

Card, grid, and timeline views

View your dates as focused countdown cards, a multi-event grid, or a visual timeline.

Calendar import

Calendar import is optional. The app previews events from the past 24 months through the next 24 months, then copies only the events you select into local Project Milepost storage.

History Packs

Twenty curated historical milestone events are bundled with the app and available from Menu > History Packs.

Advertising and Remove Ads

The free app may show a Google AdMob banner ad. The one-time Remove Ads purchase hides advertising after Apple confirms the purchase.

Local-first design

Your personal event records, notes, settings, and selected local photo copies are kept on your device instead of being sent to a Project Milepost server.
