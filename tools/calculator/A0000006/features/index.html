Math

Everyday calculation and advanced equation work

Basic

Fast arithmetic with a reusable keypad, real-time preview, and committed history for recall.

Scientific

Natural expression entry, variables and assignment, memory, trigonometric functions, logarithms, exponentials, and equation-oriented workflows.

Percentages

Segmented rows with labels, live totals, add/remove controls, and multiple chart preview modes.

Fractions

Mixed-number and improper-fraction arithmetic with addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

Visual tools

Graphing and geometry for spatial workflows

Graphing

  • Multiple functions
  • Touch-to-trace along curves
  • Approximate roots and intersections
  • Viewport controls and expression list management

Geometry

  • Common 2D shapes such as circles, rectangles, and triangles
  • Common 3D shapes such as cubes, spheres, cylinders, cones, and pyramids
  • Shape-specific dimension entry
  • Formula-driven outputs tailored to the active shape

Why this matters

These pages now market the product with concrete functionality instead of leading only with privacy boilerplate. That makes the site more suitable for a Marketing URL while remaining honest to the shipped build.

Utility

Conversions, numbers, and time

Unit conversion

Conversions across length, temperature, volume, area, speed, time, angle, power, data, pressure, energy, and force categories.

Numbers

Feature-rich number analysis, average statistics, and GCD/LCM utilities.

Time

Date/time calculations for future or past targets plus a dedicated between-dates distance workflow.

Shared settings

Theme, number formatting, percent mode, answer format, and optional haptics are stored locally to keep the experience consistent.

Finance & counting

Money, permutations, combinations, and exact counts

Money

Currency conversion plus tip, APR, auto loan, mortgage, time-value-of-money, retirement, ROI, and discount calculators.

Permutations list

List-generation workflows with support for repeated or non-repeated arrangements, subject to practical display limits.

nPr / nCr / n!

Exact combinatorics output with big-integer support for large counts.

Local and on-demand

Most workflows are local. The Money calculator only reaches out when you explicitly request the latest available exchange rate, and Home Screen widgets provide quick launch into common tools.

Shared experience

What carries across the app

Committed history

Expression/result history is stored locally for recall and review.

Touch-first layout

The interface is built around clear tap targets, adaptive layouts for supported iPhone and iPad screens, and Home Screen widgets for quick access.

Privacy posture

No account requirement, no cross-app tracking, and no third-party analytics SDKs in the current release.

Support surfaces

Privacy, support, accessibility, and privacy choices pages are all public and app-specific.
