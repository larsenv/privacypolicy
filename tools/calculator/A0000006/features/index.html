Feature guide

All shipped Great Calculator workflows in one place.

This page documents the current iPhone and iPad release in direct, product-specific language so the public website matches the live application experience.

Math

Everyday calculation and advanced equation work

Basic

Fast arithmetic with a touch-first keypad, real-time preview, clear/reset behavior, and committed history for recall.

Scientific

Natural expression entry, variables and assignment, memory, trigonometric functions, logarithms, exponentials, powers, and equation-oriented workflows.

Percentages

Segmented percentage rows with labels, live totals, add/remove controls, and multiple chart preview modes.

Fractions

Mixed-number and improper-fraction arithmetic with addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

Visual tools

Graphing and geometry for spatial workflows

Graphing

  • Multiple function entries.
  • Touch-to-trace behavior along curves.
  • Approximate roots and intersections.
  • Viewport controls and expression list management.

Geometry

  • Common 2D shapes such as circles, rectangles, and triangles.
  • Common 3D shapes such as cubes, spheres, cylinders, cones, and pyramids.
  • Shape-specific dimension entry.
  • Formula-driven outputs tailored to the active shape.
Utility

Conversions, numbers, and time

Unit conversion

Conversions across length, temperature, volume, area, speed, time, angle, power, data, pressure, energy, force, and other practical categories.

Numbers

Feature-rich number analysis, average statistics, random ranges, GCD, and LCM utilities.

Time

Date and time calculations for future or past targets, countdown-style workflows, and between-date distance calculations.

Shared settings

Theme, number formatting, percent mode, answer format, and optional haptics are stored locally to keep the experience consistent.

Finance & counting

Money, permutations, combinations, and exact counts

Money

Currency conversion plus tip, APR, auto loan, mortgage, time-value-of-money, retirement, ROI, and discount calculators.

Permutations list

List-generation workflows with support for repeated or non-repeated arrangements, subject to practical display limits.

nPr / nCr / n!

Exact combinatorics output with big-integer support for large counts.

Local and on-demand

Most workflows are local. The Money calculator only reaches out when you explicitly request the latest available exchange rate.

Shared experience

What carries across the app

Committed history

Expression/result history is stored locally for recall and review.

Touch-first layout

The interface is built around clear tap targets, adaptive layouts for supported iPhone and iPad screens, and Home Screen widgets for quick access.

Privacy posture

No account requirement, no cross-app tracking, and no third-party analytics SDKs in the current release.

Responsive roadmap

Feature requests and bug reports are actively reviewed so the app can keep improving based on real user needs.
