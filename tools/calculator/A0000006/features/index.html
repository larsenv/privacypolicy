Math

Everyday calculation and advanced equation work

Basic

Fast arithmetic with a reusable keypad, real-time preview, and committed history for recall.

Scientific

Natural expression entry, variables and assignment, memory, trigonometric functions, logarithms, exponentials, and equation-oriented workflows.

Percentages

Segmented rows with labels, live totals, and chart preview modes for common percentage workflows.

Fractions

Mixed-number and improper-fraction arithmetic with addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

Visual tools

Graphing and geometry for spatial workflows

Graphing

  • Expression, table, window, and slider panels
  • Relation graphing with y =, x =, implicit relations, inequalities, and restrictions
  • Touch-to-trace, approximate roots, intersections, and visible-window fitting
  • Multiple expression rows with per-row graph controls

Geometry

  • Common 2D shapes such as circles, rectangles, and triangles
  • Common 3D shapes such as cubes, spheres, cylinders, cones, and pyramids
  • Shape-specific dimension entry
  • Formula-driven outputs tailored to the active shape

Why this matters

The public feature guide now matches the shipped product more closely by describing the newer graphing panels, Numbers Features workflow, and Between Dates time workflow.

Utility

Conversions, numbers, time, and quick access

Unit conversion

Conversions across length, temperature, volume, area, speed, time, angle, power, data, pressure, energy, and force categories.

Numbers

Features for number properties, plus Average, GCF/LCM, and Random Picker workflows.

Time

Now-mode elapsed workflows and a Between Dates workflow with guided start and end selection.

Widgets

Home Screen quick-launch widgets for common calculator entry points.

Finance & counting

Money, permutations, combinations, and exact counts

Money

Currency conversion plus tip, APR, auto loan, mortgage, TVM, retirement, ROI, and discount calculators, with source and update disclosure for rate lookups.

Permutations list

List-generation workflows with support for repeated and non-repeated arrangements, subject to practical display limits.

nPr / nCr / n!

Exact combinatorics output with big-integer support for large counts.

Local and on-demand

Most workflows are local. The Money calculator only reaches out when you explicitly request a live rate.

Shared experience

What carries across the app

Committed history

Expression/result history is stored locally for recall and review.

Touch-first layout

The interface is built around clear tap targets and adaptive layouts for supported iPhone and iPad screens.

Privacy posture

No account requirement, no cross-app tracking, and no third-party analytics SDKs in the current release.

Public information pages

Privacy, support, accessibility, privacy choices, age suitability, content rights, and license pages are all public and app-specific.
