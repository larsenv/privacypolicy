Rights

Content and data-source rights.

Most Great Calculator workflows are computed locally by the app. The current release's notable external content path is optional exchange-rate data used by the Money calculator when a live rate is requested.

Exchange-rate source

The Money calculator can request exchange-rate data from Frankfurter at api.frankfurter.dev. The app displays the provider and available update context so users understand the source behind live conversion results.

Local formulas and calculations

Calculator formulas, unit conversions, number analysis, graphing, geometry, time, and finance helper outputs are app-generated utility results. Users remain responsible for verifying high-impact calculations before relying on them.
