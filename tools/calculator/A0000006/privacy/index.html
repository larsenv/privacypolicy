Privacy Policy

Great Calculator is designed to run primarily on your device.

This policy applies to the current iPhone and iPad release of Great Calculator. It explains what the app stores locally, what may leave the device if you choose to use optional flows, what the app does not do, and how you can manage the data that exists today.

No account required No cross-app tracking No third-party analytics SDKs Currency rates on demand only

Effective date: April 15, 2026

1. What the app stores on your device

The current release stores app settings and committed calculator history locally so the experience remains consistent between launches.

  • Preferences such as theme, number formatting, percent mode, answer format, and optional haptics
  • Committed history entries (expressions and results you choose to finalize)
  • A small local exchange-rate cache used by the Money calculator

2. What may leave the device

Money calculator live-rate requestsIf you request a live currency conversion, the app can contact api.frankfurter.dev to look up the exchange rate needed for the conversion. The source and destination currency codes are part of that request; the conversion amount is processed locally after the rate is returned.
Support emailIf you tap an email-support link and choose to send a message, your email content, attachments, and ordinary email metadata are handled by your mail provider.
Website hostingIf this public website is hosted on GitHub Pages, GitHub states that visitor IP addresses are logged and stored for security purposes. That host-level behavior applies to the website visit, not to normal calculator use inside the app.

3. What the current release does not do

  • It does not require account creation or sign-in.
  • It does not include third-party analytics SDKs.
  • It does not include advertising SDKs.
  • It does not track you across apps or websites.
  • It does not integrate with HealthKit or request Health permissions.
  • It does not enable Apple Ads attribution in the current release.

4. How the app uses information

  • Local settings are used to preserve your display and interaction preferences.
  • Committed history is used so you can review previous results in the app.
  • Local rate cache is used to reduce unnecessary repeat lookups in the Money calculator.
  • Support email is only used if you choose to contact support.

5. Retention and deletion

  • Local settings, committed history, and the local rate cache remain on the device until you change them, clear them, or remove the app.
  • If you send support email, correspondence may be retained as reasonably necessary to respond, troubleshoot, and keep basic service records, subject to legal obligations.

6. Your current privacy choices

  • Clear local history: remove saved calculator history inside the app.
  • Change local preferences: adjust theme, formatting, answer style, percent mode, and related settings.
  • Avoid live currency lookups: do not use the optional online rate path in the Money calculator.
  • Choose whether to send support email: the app does not automatically send email.
  • Remove local app data: uninstalling the app removes the app and its sandboxed local storage from the device.

7. Children

The current release is not designed to require personal information from children in order to function. The app works locally on-device and does not require account creation. If a child voluntarily sends a support email, that message is handled like any other support request.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the app’s privacy practices change, when functionality changes, or when legal or platform requirements change. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when a material revision is published.

9. Contact

For privacy questions about Great Calculator, contact trentshurts@gmail.com or review the Privacy Choices page for the direct controls available in the current release.
