Privacy Choices

Direct, practical privacy controls for the current release.

Most privacy choices in Great Calculator are straightforward because the app is primarily local. This page focuses on the actions users can actually take right now.

Read the full Privacy Policy Contact support

Current posture

  • No account to delete
  • No cross-app tracking
  • No third-party analytics SDKs
  • Live currency requests are optional

1. Clear saved history

Use the app’s history controls to remove individual history items or clear all committed history stored for recall inside the app.

2. Change local preferences

Adjust theme, formatting, percent mode, answer format, and related settings directly in the app.

3. Avoid live currency-rate requests

If you prefer not to send a one-time exchange-rate request, do not use the optional live rate lookup path in the Money calculator.

4. Choose whether to send support email

The app uses standard email links. You decide whether to open your mail app and whether to send a message or attachments.

5. Remove local app data

Uninstalling the app removes the app and its sandboxed local storage from the device, including settings, history, and the local rate cache.

6. No account deletion workflow is needed

The current release does not create or require user accounts, so there is no in-app account-deletion flow for this version.

Need more detail? The Privacy Policy explains what the app stores locally, what may leave the device in optional flows, and how data is retained.
