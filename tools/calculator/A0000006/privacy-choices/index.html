Privacy Choices

Manage Project Milepost privacy choices.

Use this page to manage advertising choices, permissions, local app data, support email, and the optional Remove Ads purchase.

Read Privacy PolicyContact Support

Ad reports

If you see an inappropriate or age-inappropriate ad, open Project Milepost and use Menu > Ads > Report an Ad. Include the approximate date and time, your country or region, a short description, and a screenshot if possible.

The public Google authorized seller file is available at https://sophiadetavex.github.io/privacypolicy/app-ads.txt.

Inside the app

These steps use normal app controls and device settings.

Advertising privacy choices

Open Project Milepost, then open Menu > Ads > Ad Privacy Choices. This appears when Google provides a choices form for your region and settings.

Remove Ads

Open Menu > Ads > Remove Ads to buy or restore the one-time Remove Ads purchase. When Apple confirms Remove Ads, banner ads are hidden.

Calendar permission

Use iOS Settings to grant or revoke Calendar access. Calendar import is optional, user-started, and does not write back to Apple Calendar.

Photo permission

Use iOS Settings to change or revoke photo-library access. You can also remove local event photo choices in the app.

Delete local events

Delete individual events in Project Milepost. Removing an event removes that event’s local title, date, notes, imported-source information, and related local metadata.

Delete all local app data

Delete the app from your device to remove its local app storage, including personal events, preferences, selected photo copies, and local recovery copies.

Support email and deletion requests

Support email is optional. Include only the details you want to share. To request deletion of a support message you sent, email trentshurts@gmail.com with enough information to find the message.

Google and Apple records

Some advertising records are handled by Google, and purchase records are handled by Apple. Use Google and Apple account or device settings for choices those companies provide directly.
