Frequently asked

Answers users and reviewers commonly need

Does Great Calculator require an account?

No. The current release does not require sign-in or account creation.

Does the app need internet access?

Most calculator workflows are local. Network access is only needed when you request a live currency-rate lookup in the Money calculator.

Where is my history stored?

Committed history and settings are stored locally on the device so they persist between launches.

What kinds of tools are included?

The shipped app includes basic, scientific, percentages, fractions, graphing, geometry, unit conversion, numbers, permutations, money, and time calculators.

How do I manage privacy choices?

Use the app’s own history/settings controls, avoid optional currency lookups, choose whether to send support email, or remove the app to remove local data.

How do I report a problem?

Email support with the calculator type, exact inputs, expected result, actual result, and app version/build if available.
