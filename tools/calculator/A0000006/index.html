What sets it apart

Commercial-grade breadth without giving up practical speed.

Great Calculator is designed for users who want one dependable calculator for school, work, budgeting, conversions, quick checks, and deeper math exploration.

Broad tool coverage

Basic, Scientific, Percentages, Fractions, Graphing, Geometry, Unit Conversion, Numbers, Permutations, Money, and Time are all available from one app shell.

Local-first privacy

Most workflows run locally. The current release does not require an account, does not include third-party analytics SDKs, and does not track users across apps or websites.

Responsive development

Support is not an afterthought. The developer reviews requests and continues shipping refinements for calculation accuracy, efficiency, and layout quality.

Adaptive iOS experience

The app is designed for iPhone and iPad, all interface orientations, light and dark mode, readable tap targets, local settings, and quick-launch widgets.

Included calculator types

One app, many workflows.

Each mode is intentionally named and documented so users can quickly understand what the app currently exposes.

Math and equations

  • Basic arithmetic with real-time preview and committed history.
  • Scientific functions, variables, memory, trigonometry, logarithms, powers, and equations.
  • Fractions and mixed-number arithmetic.
  • Percentages with live totals and chart previews.

Visual and utility tools

  • Graphing with multiple functions, trace behavior, roots, and intersections.
  • 2D and 3D geometry formulas.
  • Unit conversion across common practical categories.
  • Time calculations, countdowns, and between-date workflows.

Finance and counting

  • Currency conversion with optional on-demand exchange-rate lookup.
  • Tip, APR, auto loan, mortgage, ROI, discount, and related finance helpers.
  • Permutations, combinations, factorial, and list generation.
  • Numbers features, averages, random ranges, GCD, and LCM.

Trust surfaces

  • Dedicated privacy policy and privacy choices pages.
  • Public support channel with a reproducible report template.
  • Accessibility, age-suitability, license, and content-rights pages.
  • Clear explanations of optional network behavior.
