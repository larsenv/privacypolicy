Subtitle: Date Tracker & Countdown

Core features

One timeline for future plans and past memories.

Project Milepost is designed for people who want a simple way to see meaningful dates clearly without creating an account or sending personal event records to a Project Milepost server.

Countdowns and elapsed time

See how long remains until upcoming milestones and how much time has passed since past events.

Milestones and memories

Track birthdays, anniversaries, goals, deadlines, memories, life events, and personal dates.

Optional Calendar import

Preview Calendar events you choose, then copy selected events into Project Milepost.

Guided tour

Learn the app through typed instruction bubbles, connector lines, and precise highlights on one control at a time.

Single-event Home Screen widget

Add one read-only widget that shows a single event with its Project Milepost color or image background and count-up or countdown information, including weeks and days.

Local event photos

Add optional photos from your device to personalize the events that matter most.

History included

Enable 20 bundled historical milestones covering science, medicine, computing, law, and space.

Optional Remove Ads

The app may show banner ads. A one-time Remove Ads purchase hides banner ads after confirmation.
